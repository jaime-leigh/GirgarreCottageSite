About

The Girgarre Community Cottage (GCC) is a safe space that has been providing fun, laughter, friendship and learning for 30 years and is looking forward to the next 30 and more! Join classes, learn skills & access holiday activities. The Community Centre exists to help, support and encourage people of this rural area to enrich their lives by developing friendships and new learning skills.
