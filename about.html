About

The Girgarre Community Cottage (GCC) is a safe space that has been providing friendship, learning, help, fun and laughter for 30 years and is looking forward to the next 30 years and more! Join classes, learn skills and socialise with other community members.

We are here to ensure community members have access to all essential services needed to thrive. If we cannot assist we can connect with people, groups, organisations that can. Confidentiality is ensured. No problem, issue or celebrations is too big or too small.

Our Mission

The Community Centre exists to help, support and encourage people of this rural area to enrich their lives by developing friendships and new learning skills.

Our Child Safe Commitment

GCC are committed to creating an environment for children and young people to be safe and feel safe. We commit to using best practice standards in the recruitment, screening, and employment of GCC employees and volunteers so they will not harm, abuse or exploit children and young people who are involved in our programs, services and facilities.

How You Can Help

If you would like to help out at the Cottage, raise funds for worthy causes, teach a class, or be on our Committee of Management, please drop in and see Kezia to find out more. Your commitment can be short or long-term, whatever suits you! Come in and join us; you’ll be glad you did!
