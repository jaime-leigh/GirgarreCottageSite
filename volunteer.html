Volunteer with us

We are always looking for wonderful people to volunteer some time toward one of the many services or activities we provide.

All our volunteers are highly valued. It would be impossible to run the cottage and provide services to the community without their help. We can tailor something to your special skills or interests.

Please contact the Girgarre Community Cottage on (03) 5854 6482 if you are interested in volunteering.
