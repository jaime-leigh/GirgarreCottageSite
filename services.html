Community
Car

X

The car is available to transport community members to medical appointments locally or further afield to Melbourne or Bendigo. All it takes is a call to the Girgarre Cottage with the details and we arrange the driver. The car is driven by volunteers who will take the passenger to their appointment, wait for them, then drive home again. There is a charge for this service. More drivers are always needed. Full induction is provided and a meal allowance is given to the driver for longer trips. Please call the Cottage on 03 5854 6482 to enquire about becoming a volunteer driver.

Socialising

X

Each week Girgarre Seniors meet up to socialise and have lunch either onsite at the Girgarre Community Cottage or hop on a bus for an outing offsite. Lovely meals are cooked and delivered to community members that may be ill or incapacitated and confined to their homes. Community
Garden

X

The Happy Harvest Community Garden, in the front yard of the Cottage, provides fresh produce available to all community members. The Garden is maintained by volunteers and funded by grants and donations.

Container
Deposit Scheme

X

Girgarre Community Cottage is a donation partner under the CDS. By donating eligible containers to the Cottage, the community benefits from the 10c refund. Follow Girgarre Community Cottage on Facebook or check out CDSVIC.ORG.AU for information on eligible containers
