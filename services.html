Community Car

The car is available to transport community members to medical appointments locally or further afield to Melbourne or Bendigo. All it takes is a call to the Girgarre Cottage with the details and we arrange the driver. The car is driven by volunteers who will take the passenger to their appointment, wait for them, then drive home again. There is a charge for this service.

Computer Assistance

A computer hub is available for use by community members free of cost (except small charges for downloads/printing) or we can help out with computer needs / technical help.


Meals and Socialising

LEach week Girgarre Seniors meet up to socialise and have lunch either onsite at the Girgarre Community Cottage or hop on a bus for an outing offsite. Lovely meals are cooked and delivered to community members that may be ill or incapacitated and confined to their homes. Also, we organise and cater for special gatherings/events, for example, the Australia Day Awards or Australiaâ€™s Biggest Morning Tea.


Food Assistance

We provide food parcels for community members on request. These parcels may include fresh produce, frozen food/meals, pantry items and toiletries.


Volunteer With Us

We are always looking for wonderful people to volunteer some time toward one of the many services or activities we provide. All our volunteers are highly valued as it would be impossible to run the cottage and provide services to the community without their help. Please contact the Girgarre Community Cottage on (03) 5854 6482 if you are interested in volunteering.
